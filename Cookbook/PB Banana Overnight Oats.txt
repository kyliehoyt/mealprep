PB Banana Overnight Oats
Breakfast
10 cal | 5 P | 2 C | 4 F
Ingredients
Q	unit	ingredient
1.5	un	banana
3	tbsp	peanut butter
1.25	cup	milk
0.25	cup	yogurt
1.5	tbsp	honey
1.25	cup	rolled oats
Recipe
1. Mash bananas in a big bowl
2. Mix in the peanut butter, yogurt, milk, and honey until smooth
3. Add in the rolled oats and cinnamon and mix again
4. Refrigerate covered overnight
