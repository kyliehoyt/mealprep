Spicy Shrimp Spaghetti
Dinner
 486 cal | 39 prot | 58 carb | 12 fat Ingredients
quantity	unit	ingredient
14	oz	protein spaghetti
2	un	chili pepper
2	un	zucchini
24	oz	shrimp
0.75	cup	shredded mozzarela cheese
3	tbsp	unsalted butter
Recipe
1. Cook spaghetti. Reserve 1/4 cup pasta water
2. Cut zucchinis into half moons, thinly slice chilis, rinse shrimp and pat very dry
3. Heat a large drizzle of oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and sliced chili. Cook 2-3 min.
4. Push zucchini to the side and add a large drizzle of oil and shrimp to the other side. Cook until pink and just cooked through, 3-4 min
5. Add spaghetti, garlic herb butter, half the parmesan, and pasta water to the pan. Season generously and toss until combined.
6. Top with remaining parmesan, a drizzle of oil and crushed red pepper.
